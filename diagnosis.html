6. Diagnosis

Diagnosis of epilepsy involves detailed medical history analysis and neurological examinations to identify the cause and type of seizures.

  • Electroencephalogram (EEG): Records electrical activity of the brain.
  • MRI or CT Scans: Help detect structural abnormalities or brain damage.
  • Blood Tests: Identify infections, metabolic disorders, or other medical conditions.

Early and accurate diagnosis helps doctors choose the most effective treatment plan and improve patient outcomes.
