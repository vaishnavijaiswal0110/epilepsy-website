9. Prevention and Safety

Epilepsy cannot always be prevented, but certain safety measures can help reduce risks and protect individuals during seizures.

Safety Measures

  • Wearing a medical identification bracelet or card to provide emergency information
  • Avoiding hazardous activities alone such as swimming, climbing heights, or operating heavy machinery
  • Educating family members, friends, and caregivers about seizure first aid and emergency response

Taking these precautions helps improve safety and supports better management of epilepsy.
