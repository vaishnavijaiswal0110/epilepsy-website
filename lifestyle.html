8. Lifestyle and Management

Lifestyle changes can help reduce seizure frequency and improve overall health for individuals with epilepsy.

  • Regular sleep patterns
  • Stress management
  • Avoiding seizure triggers

Maintaining a healthy routine, following prescribed medications, and managing stress levels can help individuals with epilepsy live safer and more productive lives.
